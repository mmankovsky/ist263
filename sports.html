Sports Vehicles

Chevrolet Corvette Starting at $62,195 The Chevrolet Corvette is one of the best and known sports cars for the almost the last 70 years. The Corvette is the ultimate American sports car and is the dream of many car enthusiasts to own one one day. The all new 2022 chevy corvette has brought in all new super car performance while still having a luxurious cabin and daily driving capabilities. The Corvette has always been a sports car that has been a bit more affordable than others while still having the same performance abilities. What makes the corvette not as appealing than other sports cars is that it is American made and doesn’t have the foreign engineers that a super car needs.

Audi R8 Starting at $147,995 The Audi R8 has always been the ultimate four wheel drive sports car. Being that the car is not rear wheel drive all four wheels spin at the same time so when the car accelerates it gets off the line quicker because it is able to catch grip. The car has always had a very eye catching look for an Audi because it is a super car somewhat. Audi and the R8 are also very special because that car has a V10 engine which is very rare in a car. Most sports cars have a V8 and V12 engines. But that is why the R8 is so special. Audi has always been known for its special engines just like their 5 cylinder that nobody else carries

Porsche 911 Starting at $114,000 The Porsche 911 is just slightly above the Mercedes SL and the Chevy Corvette as the ultimate sports cars. The Porsche may be above them because it is a Porsche, but they are so exclusive for that reason. The 911 is one of those vehicles that are just as great 10 years ago and now. The 911s hold their value like no other vehicle. You can purchase one and 10 years later sell it for more than half of what you paid for it. This car has been around since the 60’s and it isn’t going anywhere ever. These types of vehicles are statement vehicles that are just considered more valuable driving and money wise.
